Stir-fry

Description

A favorite family recipe that my Mom has made for hundreds of guests. Fresh lemon juice and mint leaves are a must! Serve over hot cooked white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken
  • clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ½ cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps in Making Stir Fry

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with garlic. In a small bowl, combine flour and margarine. Pour over chicken, and coat with lemon juice and mint.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour, or until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Serve hot.
